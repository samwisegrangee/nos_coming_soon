About

Inspired by Douglas Hofstadter's book “Godel, Escher, Bach” Dr. Dan Kennedy began exploring the practical and philosophical significance of self-referencing. Dr. Kennedy’s ideas evolved into a robust paradigm. An original mode of thinking that enables a fusion type effect on the world. Creating more than consuming.

That dynamic became known as “Syntropy Thotware.” Dr. Kennedy used his creativity to envision a future full of promise and peril. A future where every citizen of earth could be also be part of a new nation, the Nation of Space. Through various media projects throughout the years he has sought to give people an experience of how the universe expands and evolves. This has included a radio drama, the comics found on this site, and more recently a series of short films the first of which is now debuting in film festivals throughout the country.

NationofSpace.com is the home of all these projects. Through “Q” a direct exchange can be made with a better future through a more pleasant present by way of educational entertainment. Explore and enjoy!
